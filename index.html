📥 Monthly Contributions
1
PF (EPF): Employee 12% of Basic + Employer 12% of Basic. Interest: 8.25% p.a., compounded half-yearly (Mar & Sep)
2
PPF: Fixed monthly deposit. Interest: 7.1% p.a., credited yearly on 5th-to-last-day balance
3
NPS: Employee + Employer contributions. Assumed return: 10% p.a. compounded monthly
4
VPF / Others: Additional voluntary PF over 12% limit, earns same PF rate
All contributions compound to final corpus shown in the Monthly Timeline page

🎁 Retirement Benefits
1
Gratuity: (Basic/26) × 15 × years_of_service. Max ₹20L tax-free. Formula: Last Basic ÷ 26 × 15 × service_years
2
Leave Encashment: EL balance × (Basic + DA)/26. Max 300 days for central govt. Formula: EL days × (Basic+DA)/26
3
Commutation of Pension: Up to 40% of pension × commutation factor (age-based). Formula: 40% × monthly pension × 12 × commutation_factor
4
Others: LTC, group insurance, superannuation fund — enter manually
